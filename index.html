Jack Vu

I am a senior at St. John's school interested in using computer science to navigate the fields of linguistics and Latin American studies.

I founded and currently run a program called Digital Doorways that aims to provide public computer centers and computer literacy education for underserved Houston communities. With upwards of $34,900 in funds and materials raised, Digital Doorways has constructed three computer labs that serve 150+ students. Our partners include Baker-Ripley, the YMCA of Greater Houston, Mission Milby CDC, Christian Community Service Center, and the St. Austin Literacy Center.

Aside from computer education, I am deeply interested in building tools to solve pressing issues.
