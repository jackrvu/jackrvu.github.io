Jack Vu

I am a senior at St. John's school in Houston interested in Latin American studies and computer science. I founded and currently run a program called Digital Doorways that provides public computer centers and computer literacy education to underserved Houston communities. To date, the program has constructed and maintained three computer labs that serve 100+ students weekly.

I am deeply interested in building tools to solve pressing issues.

Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in CS from Stanford (advised by Peter Bailis and Philip Levis) and my B.S. in CS from Caltech.

I am actively looking for master and PhD students. If you are a GT student who is interested in working with me, please check out this page.
