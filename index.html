Jack Vu

I am a senior at St. John's school in Houston interested in computer science and Latin American studies.

I founded and currently run a program called Digital Doorways that provides public computer centers and computer literacy education to underserved Houston communities. To date, the program has constructed and maintained three computer labs that serve 100+ students weekly.

Aside from computer education, I am deeply interested in building tools to solve pressing issues.
